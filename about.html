Test Yourself

With Test Yourself, you can build your own multiple-choice quizzes.


This includes:

  • Naming your quiz
  • Writing your questions
  • Getting feedback on your answers

Quiz results use positive marking.


Scores are generated depending on how many right answers your user selects in total.


That means you can have:

  • Multiple right answers
  • No right answers
  • One right answer
